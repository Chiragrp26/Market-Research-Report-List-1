<p><p>A sandwich automotive heat shield is a protective component used in automobiles to shield the vehicle's engine, exhaust system, and other components from excessive heat. It comprises of multiple layers of material, including aluminum, stainless steel, or ceramic, which are assembled in a sandwich-like structure. These heat shields act as a barrier between the hot parts of the vehicle and other sensitive areas, preventing heat damage and ensuring optimal performance.</p><p>The trend of using sandwich automotive heat shields has been growing rapidly in recent years due to the increasing demand for high-performance vehicles worldwide. The automotive industry has seen significant advancements in engine technology, resulting in increased heat generation.
