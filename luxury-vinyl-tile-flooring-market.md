<p><p>The Luxury Vinyl Tile Flooring market is segmented into different types, including SPC (Stone Polymer Composite), WPC (Wood Polymer Composite), and others. SPC flooring is made of a stone polymer composite core, which provides high durability and stability. WPC flooring, on the other hand, is composed of a wood polymer composite core, offering a balance between durability and comfort. Both SPC and WPC flooring are waterproof and resistant to scratches, making them suitable for various environments. The "Others" category includes different types of luxury vinyl tile flooring that may have unique compositions or features not covered by SPC or WPC.</p></p>
