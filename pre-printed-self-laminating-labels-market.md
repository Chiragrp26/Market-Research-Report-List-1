<p><p>The pre-printed self-laminating labels market is categorized based on the technology used to create these labels. Laser technology involves using a laser printer to print the label design onto a blank label sheet, which is then laminated to protect the print. Thermal technology utilizes a thermal printer to transfer heat onto a thermal laminate, resulting in a laminated label. The "others" category includes alternative technologies such as inkjet printing or screen printing, which are less common but still used to create self-laminating labels.</p></p>
