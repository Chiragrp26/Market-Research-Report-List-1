<p><p>The automatic rotary indexing tables market consists of three main types: servo-driven gear head, servo-driven barrel-cam rotary indexing table, and cam-driven indexing rotary indexing table. Servo-driven gear head tables use a servo motor to achieve precise positioning and high-speed operation. Servo-driven barrel-cam tables utilize a barrel-cam mechanism combined with a servo motor to ensure accurate and smooth indexing motions. Cam-driven indexing tables employ a cam mechanism to achieve indexing movements at predetermined intervals. All three types offer unique features and cater to different applications in various industries.</p></p>
