<p><p>Microporous breathable films are widely used in various industries due to their unique properties. Microporous PE films are made of polyethylene and have excellent moisture vapor transmission rate (MVTR) properties, allowing them to effectively control moisture levels and provide breathability. Microporous PP films, on the other hand, are made of polypropylene and exhibit similar breathability characteristics. Other types of microporous films may include those made from materials like polyester or copolymer blends. These films offer breathability while maintaining protection against liquids, making them suitable for applications such as medical devices, protective clothing, and packaging materials.</p></p>
