<p><ul><li>Autosamplers for Liquids</li><li>Autosamplers for Solids</li></ul></p>
<p><p>TOC Autosamplers are devices used in analytical chemistry to automate the process of sample introduction into a Total Organic Carbon (TOC) analyzer. There are two types of TOC Autosamplers commonly used in the market: Autosamplers for Liquids and Autosamplers for Solids. </p><p>Autosamplers for Liquids are designed to handle liquid samples and are equipped with features like sample tray, syringe needle, and sample vial, allowing for efficient and precise injection of liquid samples into the TOC analyzer. </p><p>On the other hand, Autosamplers for Solids are specifically designed to handle solid samples. These autosamplers enable the automation of sample introduction by providing mechanisms to handle and prepare solid samples for measurement by the TOC analyzer.</p></p>
