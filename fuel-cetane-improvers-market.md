Nitrates are one type of cetane improver that releases oxygen during combustion, resulting in improved ignition quality. Organic peroxides work by decomposing into free radicals which promote faster combustion. Organosulfur compounds enhance cetane levels by increasing the fuel's lubricity and reducing emissions. The "Other" category includes miscellaneous additives like metal alkyls and metallo-organic compounds, which improve cetane levels through different mechanisms.</p></p>
