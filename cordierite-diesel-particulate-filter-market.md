<p><p>Cordierite Diesel Particulate Filters (DPFs) can be classified into two types: Active and Passive. Active DPFs use a fuel burner to increase the temperature of the filter and facilitate the combustion of particulate matter, reducing emissions. These filters are typically used in vehicles that operate under high load and temperature conditions. On the other hand, Passive DPFs rely on the natural exhaust temperature to remove particulate matter, making them suitable for vehicles with lower temperature operations. Both types play a crucial role in reducing harmful emissions from diesel engines, but they differ in their operation and application.</p></p>
