<p><p>The Paclitaxel-eluting stent market consists of three major types: metal stents, polymer stents, and others. Metal stents are made of materials like stainless steel or cobalt-chromium alloy, providing structural support to the blocked arteries. Polymer stents, on the other hand, incorporate a biocompatible polymer coating along with the drug that helps in reducing inflammation and restenosis.
