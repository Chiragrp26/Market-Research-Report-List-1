<p><strong>The Roof Garden Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Intensive Roof Garden</li><li>Extensive Roof Garden</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Roof gardens can be classified into two main types: intensive and extensive. Intensive roof gardens involve the cultivation of a wide variety of plants, including trees, shrubs, and even vegetables. They require more maintenance and attention due to their greater weight, depth of soil, and the need for irrigation systems. On the other hand, extensive roof gardens involve lightweight vegetation, like groundcovers and small grasses. They require less maintenance and have shallower soil, making them more suitable for buildings with weight restrictions.</p></p>
