<p><p>The automotive signal lamps market can be categorized into three main types: traditional lighting, LED lighting, and other markets. LED lighting, on the other hand, utilizes light-emitting diodes for enhanced brightness and energy efficiency. The "other market" refers to alternative lighting technologies that are not as prevalent as traditional or LED lighting, such as halogen or xenon lamps. These different types cater to varied customer preferences and technological advancements in the automotive industry.</p></p>
