<p><p>Semiconductor wafer transfer robots are automated systems used in the semiconductor industry to handle and transfer delicate wafers during manufacturing processes. There are two types of these robots available in the market: atmospheric manipulators and vacuum manipulators. Atmospheric manipulators use grippers or end-effectors to handle wafers in ambient air conditions, while vacuum manipulators rely on vacuum suction cups to securely hold wafers during transfer. Both types of robots offer precise and efficient handling of wafers, ensuring their safety and preventing any potential damage.</p></p>
