<p><p>Polyimide film is a type of heat-resistant film used in the automotive industry. There are two main methods used to produce this film - chemical imidization and thermal imidization. Chemical imidization involves the reaction of a polyamic acid solution with a chemical agent to form the final film. On the other hand, thermal imidization is a process where the polymer film is heated to a high temperature to convert it into a polyimide film. Both methods result in a durable and high-performance film suitable for various automotive applications.</p></p>
