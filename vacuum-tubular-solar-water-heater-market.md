<p><p>The Vacuum Tubular Solar Water Heater market is categorized into three main types: All Glass Vacuum Tube, Heat Pipe Vacuum Tube, and U-shaped Vacuum Tube. </p><p>All Glass Vacuum Tubes are made of high-quality glass materials and provide excellent insulation, resulting in efficient heat absorption and retention. Heat Pipe Vacuum Tubes contain a copper heat pipe that transfers heat from the absorber to the condenser using the phase change of the working fluid, ensuring superior heat transfer efficiency. U-shaped Vacuum Tubes are designed with a U-shaped structure, enhancing their absorption area and allowing for better heat absorption even under low solar radiation conditions.</p></p>
