<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The automotive catalytic converter market consists of different types of devices. Three-way catalysts (TWC) are designed to reduce harmful emissions like carbon monoxide, nitrogen oxides, and hydrocarbons. Lean NOx catalysts (LNC) specifically target the reduction of nitrogen oxides during lean fuel conditions. Lean NOx traps (LNT) temporarily store and subsequently reduce nitrogen oxides. Selective catalytic reduction (SCR) systems use a catalyst to convert nitrogen oxides into harmless nitrogen and water. Diesel oxidation catalysts (DOC) are used in diesel vehicles to reduce carbon monoxide and hydrocarbon emissions.</p></p>
