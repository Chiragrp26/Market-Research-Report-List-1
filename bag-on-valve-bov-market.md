<p><p>Bag On Valve (BOV) is a packaging technology used in various industries. The market types of BOV include Continuous BOV, Metered BOV, and Others. Continuous BOV allows continuous dispensing of the product manufactured, making it ideal for products that require continuous spraying, such as insecticides and cleaners. Metered BOV, on the other hand, controls the amount of product dispensed with each spray, suitable for applications like pharmaceuticals and cosmetics. The "Others" category encompasses other variations and adaptations of BOV technology, tailored to specific industry needs, like food packaging or personal care products.</p></p>
