<p><ul><li>Parallel Hybrid Drivetrains</li><li>Series Hybrid Drivetrains</li><li>Series-Parallel Hybrid Drivetrains</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hybrid vehicle drivetrains market is divided into three types: parallel hybrid drivetrains, series hybrid drivetrains, and series-parallel hybrid drivetrains. In parallel hybrid drivetrains, both the internal combustion engine and electric motor power the vehicle directly. Series hybrid drivetrains use an internal combustion engine solely to charge the electric motor, which in turn powers the vehicle. Series-parallel hybrid drivetrains combine characteristics of both parallel and series hybrid drivetrains, allowing the vehicle to operate on both electric power and with the internal combustion engine. These drivetrains offer different benefits and suit varying driving conditions.</p></p>
