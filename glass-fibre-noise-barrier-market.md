<p><p>Glass Fibre Noise Barrier market includes two main types: ground-mounted noise barriers and structure-mounted noise barriers. Ground-mounted noise barriers are installed on the ground and are commonly used along highways and railways to reduce noise pollution for nearby residential areas. These barriers are typically made of glass fiber and provide an effective sound insulation solution. On the other hand, structure-mounted noise barriers are installed directly on buildings, such as bridges and overpasses, to reduce noise from passing vehicles. These noise barriers are also made of glass fiber and offer a durable and efficient noise reduction solution.</p></p>
