<p><p>Communications-Based Train Control (CBTC) is a signaling system utilized in railways to enhance train control and management. Basic CBTC employs continuous communication between the train and trackside equipment, allowing for real-time monitoring and control. It offers improved safety, reliability, capacity, and flexibility. On the other hand, I-CBTC, or Integrated CBTC, integrates additional functionalities like train planning, scheduling, and optimization. I-CBTC leverages advanced technologies such as artificial intelligence and machine learning to enhance operational efficiency and provide a seamless passenger experience. Both systems aim to revolutionize railway operations and improve overall performance.</p></p>
