<p><p>Ketone resin is a common type of synthetic resin used in various industries. It can be categorized into three main types based on its chemical composition: Carbon Chain Polymer, Hetero Chain Polymer, and Element Organic Polymer. Carbon Chain Polymer refers to ketone resin composed of carbon atoms primarily linked together. Hetero Chain Polymer consists of heteroatoms (such as oxygen or sulfur) in addition to carbon atoms. Element Organic Polymer contains additional organic elements, like nitrogen or silicon, apart from carbon atoms. These different types of ketone resin cater to specific application needs in industries like adhesives, coatings, and inks.</p></p>
