There are various types of hot-melt adhesives available in the market. EVA HMA, SBC HMA, PA HMA, APAO HMA, POE HMA, and other market types are commonly used. EVA HMA refers to Ethylene Vinyl Acetate-based adhesives, SBC HMA to Styrene-Butadiene Copolymer-based, PA HMA to Polyamide-based, APAO HMA to Amorphous Poly Alpha Olefin-based, and POE HMA refers to Polyolefin Elastomer-based adhesives. Each type has its own unique properties and characteristics suitable for specific packaging requirements.</p></p>
