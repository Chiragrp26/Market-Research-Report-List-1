<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The IC photoresist market can be categorized into five types - ArF Immersion, ArF Dry Film, KrF, G-LINE/I-LINE, and Others. ArF Immersion refers to photoresists used for immersion lithography using ArF wavelength; ArF Dry Film is a type of photoresist that does not require a liquid immersion process. KrF refers to photoresists used in lithography processes using a KrF excimer laser. G-LINE/I-LINE photoresists are for older generation lithography techniques. Lastly, "Others" refers to photoresists used in special applications or with unique features that don't fit into the other categories.</p></p>
