<p><p>The market for front lights in vehicles can be divided into three main types: Xenon Lights, Halogen Lights, and LED lights. Xenon lights use a technology that produces a bright and white light, offering better visibility especially during nighttime driving. Halogen lights, on the other hand, use a tungsten filament and emit a warm, yellowish light. They are commonly found in most vehicles due to their cost-effectiveness. LED lights, the most advanced option, use light-emitting diodes to produce bright and efficient lighting, with the added advantage of longer lifespan and energy efficiency.</p></p>
