<p><p>The OSDF (Oral Solid Dosage Forms) excipients market includes different types of excipients used in the production of powder, granules, tablets, capsules, and dropping pills. Powder excipients are fine particles that enhance the flowability and compressibility of the formulation. Granules are larger particles formed by agglomeration, improving the compressibility and uniformity of the dosage form. Tablets are solid, compressed doses containing active ingredients and excipients. Capsules consist of gelatin shells enclosing powder or granules. Dropping pills are prepared through a process that allows liquid droplets to be solidified, forming solid dosage forms.</p></p>
