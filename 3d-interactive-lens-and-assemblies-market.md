<p><p>The 3D Interactive Lens and Assemblies market encompasses different types, such as the Structured Light Method and Time of Flight Method (ToF). In the Structured Light Method, a pattern of light is projected onto the object, and the distortion of the pattern is used to calculate the object's depth and shape. On the other hand, the Time of Flight Method measures the time taken for light to travel to the object and back, enabling the calculation of the distance and shape of the object. Both methods play a crucial role in the development of interactive 3D technology and related applications.</p></p>
