<p><p>PTFE screws market is categorized into four types based on the screw head design. Slotted screws feature a single slot on the head for tightening or loosening using a flat-blade screwdriver. Cross recessed screws have a cross-shaped recess on the head, enabling easy tightening with a Phillips or Pozidriv screwdriver. Hexagon socket screws have a hexagonal socket on the head, enabling tightening with an Allen key. The "Others" category encompasses screws with unique head designs or combinations of different design features to suit specific applications.</p></p>
