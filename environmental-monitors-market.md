<p><p>The environmental monitors market includes two main types: portable and stationary monitors. They are often used for personal exposure monitoring or assessing air quality in specific areas. On the other hand, stationary monitors are permanent fixtures installed in specific locations to continuously measure and monitor air quality or environmental conditions. They provide long-term data collection and are commonly used in industrial settings, research facilities, or heavily populated areas. Both types of monitors play a crucial role in detecting and analyzing environmental factors to ensure a healthier and safer environment.</p></p>
