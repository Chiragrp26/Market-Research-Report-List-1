<p><p>Thin section bearings are a type of specialized bearings that are designed to have a small cross-section, making them suitable for applications where space is limited. There are several types of thin section bearings available in the market. Radial ball bearings are most commonly used and are designed to support radial loads. Angular contact bearings, on the other hand, can handle both radial and axial loads. Four-point contact bearings have four points of contact, providing increased load-carrying capacity. Other types of thin section bearings include thrust bearings, which can handle axial loads, and airframe control bearings, which are used in aerospace applications.</p></p>
