<p><p>Artificial fiber refers to man-made fibers that are derived from various materials like chemicals, petroleum, or natural sources. The artificial fiber market can be categorized into two types: filament fiber and staple fibers. Filament fibers are long continuous strands of fiber that are used in industries like textiles, upholstery, and carpets. On the other hand, staple fibers are short lengths of fiber that are commonly used in the production of fabrics for clothing, bedding, and non-woven materials. Both filament and staple fibers play vital roles in the artificial fiber market, catering to different industries and applications.</p></p>
