Vitamin E, a fat-soluble antioxidant found in nuts and vegetable oils, protects cell membranes from oxidative damage. Carotenoids, such as lycopene and beta-carotene, are found in colorful fruits and vegetables and help protect against chronic diseases. Polyphenols, found in foods like berries and dark chocolate, have diverse antioxidant properties and contribute to overall health.</p></p>
