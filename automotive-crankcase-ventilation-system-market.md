<p><p>The Automotive Crankcase Ventilation System Market includes three main types: Crank Case Ventilation, Catch Can Placement, and Correct PCV Hose Routing. Crank Case Ventilation refers to the process of removing harmful gases from the engine's crankcase and replacing them with fresh air. Catch Can Placement involves the installation of a catch can, which separates oil particles from the intake manifold to prevent engine contamination. Finally, Correct PCV Hose Routing ensures that the Positive Crankcase Ventilation (PCV) system is correctly connected and functioning to maintain optimal engine performance and emissions control.</p></p>
