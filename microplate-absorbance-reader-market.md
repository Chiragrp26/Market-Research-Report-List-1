<p><p>The microplate absorbance reader market can be categorized into two types: single-mode and multimode readers. Single-mode microplate readers are designed to measure absorbance in a specific wavelength range, making them suitable for applications that require analysis of a single type of assay. On the other hand, multimode microplate readers offer versatility as they can measure absorbance in multiple wavelength ranges, allowing for the analysis of various assays. This makes multimode readers more flexible and ideal for labs that perform different types of assays.</p></p>
