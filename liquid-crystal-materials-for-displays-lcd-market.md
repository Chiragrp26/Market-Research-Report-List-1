STN (Super Twisted Nematic) type liquid crystal materials have better contrast and wider viewing angles. HTN (High-Temperature Nematic) type liquid crystal materials are used in displays that require increased heat tolerance. These materials cater to different display requirements, offering varying benefits and features.</p></p>
