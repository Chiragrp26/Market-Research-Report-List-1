<p><p>Threaded solenoid valve market types include 2-way, 3-way, 5-way, and other variations. A 2-way solenoid valve consists of an inlet and outlet, controlling the flow of a fluid through a single path. A 3-way solenoid valve, on the other hand, has three openings to regulate the flow between two different paths. A 5-way solenoid valve offers five ports which allow for even more complex flow control, typically found in advanced applications. The "other" category encompasses various specialized solenoid valves tailored to specific industry requirements, such as high-pressure applications or those requiring specific materials of construction.</p></p>
