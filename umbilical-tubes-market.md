Super-duplex stainless steel has high corrosion resistance and strength, making it suitable for demanding offshore applications. Hyper-duplex stainless steel, on the other hand, offers even greater corrosion resistance and strength, making it ideal for extreme environments with high levels of chloride, hydrogen sulfide, or sour gases.
