<p><p>Corona treatment equipment is a type of surface treatment equipment used to enhance the adhesion properties of plastic films, foils, and other materials in industries such as packaging, automotive, printing, and electronics. This equipment works by creating an electric discharge, or corona, which ionizes the air around the material, resulting in improved surface energy and wettability. This treatment increases the bonding capabilities of the material, allowing better adhesion of inks, coatings, or adhesives.</p><p>The market analysis of the corona treatment equipment industry suggests significant growth in recent years and a positive outlook for the future. Factors driving this market growth include the increasing demand for plastic films in various industries, the growing preference for eco-friendly and sustainable packaging solutions, and the rising awareness about surface treatment technologies to improve product quality.</p><p>Additionally, the market for corona treatment equipment is fueled by advancements in technology, such as the development of advanced high-frequency corona treatment systems, which provide better treatment and wider web coverage.
