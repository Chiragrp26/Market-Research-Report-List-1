<p><strong>The Outdoor Pool Heat Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Geothermal Heat Pumps</li><li>Air source Heat Pumps</li><li>Water source Heat Pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The outdoor pool heat pumps market includes three major types: Geothermal heat pumps, air source heat pumps, and water source heat pumps. Geothermal heat pumps utilize the earth's natural warmth to heat pools efficiently. Air source heat pumps draw heat from the outside air and transfer it to the pool water. Water source heat pumps use a water source, such as a nearby lake or river, as a heat exchange medium. All three types offer effective pool heating solutions, with geothermal and water source heat pumps being more energy-efficient options.</p></p>
