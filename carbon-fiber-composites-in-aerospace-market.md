<p><p>Carbon fiber composites in the aerospace market can be categorized into two types: thermosetting and thermoplastic. Thermosetting types are formed through a curing process, where the resin hardens irreversibly. They possess high strength and stiffness, and are widely used in structural applications in aerospace. On the other hand, thermoplastic types can be melted and reshaped multiple times without losing their properties. They offer good impact resistance and are suitable for applications requiring toughness and weight reduction. Both types are utilized in the aerospace industry for their unique characteristics and performance capabilities.</p></p>
