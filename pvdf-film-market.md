<p><p>The PVDF film market can be divided into three main types based on the orientation of the film. Uniaxially oriented film is a type of PVDF film that is stretched in one direction, resulting in enhanced mechanical properties and excellent dimensional stability. Biaxially oriented film is stretched in both the machine direction and the transverse direction, leading to improved tensile strength, toughness, and barrier properties. The "others" segment includes PVDF films that do not fall into the mentioned categories, possibly due to different manufacturing processes or unique characteristics.</p></p>
