<p><ul><li>Negative Type</li><li>Positive Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconducting glass market can be categorized into two types: negative type and positive type. Negative type semiconducting glass exhibits conductivity when exposed to light, while positive type semiconducting glass shows conductivity when light is blocked. Negative type market pertains to semiconducting glass used in devices that require light exposure, such as sensors, solar cells, and optical switches. Positive type market, on the other hand, is focused on applications where light needs to be blocked, like light detectors and displays. The difference lies in the behavior of these glasses towards light exposure.</p></p>
