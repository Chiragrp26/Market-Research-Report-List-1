<p><p>Aircraft weighing equipment market can be categorized into two types: platform and floor-standing. Platform weighing equipment refers to devices that are placed on the ground and used to measure the weight of an aircraft. These platforms are usually portable and can be easily moved around. On the other hand, floor-standing weighing equipment is fixed to the ground and designed to handle heavier aircraft. They are more stable and provide accurate readings due to their robust construction. Both types of equipment play a crucial role in ensuring safety and compliance in the aviation industry.</p></p>
