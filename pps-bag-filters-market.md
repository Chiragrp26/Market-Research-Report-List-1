<p><p>The PPS Bag Filters market is divided into three types: Mechanical Shaker Bag Filter, Reverse Air Bag Filter, and Pulse Jet Bag Filter. The Mechanical Shaker Bag Filter operates by shaking the bags to remove the dust particles. The Reverse Air Bag Filter uses a reverse air flow to clean the bags. The Pulse Jet Bag Filter shoots short bursts of compressed air to clean the bags. These different types cater to various industrial applications and offer efficient filtration solutions for the removal of dust and pollutants.</p></p>
