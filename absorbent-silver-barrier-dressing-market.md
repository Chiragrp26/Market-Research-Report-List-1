<p><p>The absorbent silver barrier dressing market consists of two types: adhesive absorbent silver barrier dressing and non-adhesive absorbent silver barrier dressing. Adhesive absorbent silver barrier dressings are designed with a self-adhesive backing, making them easy to apply and secure on wounds. They provide a protective barrier against bacteria while absorbing and locking in excess moisture. Non-adhesive absorbent silver barrier dressings, on the other hand, do not have a self-adhesive backing and are typically held in place with a secondary dressing or tape. Both types offer the benefits of silver technology, which helps prevent infection and promote wound healing.</p></p>
