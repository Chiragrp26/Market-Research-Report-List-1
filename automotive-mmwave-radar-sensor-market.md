<p><p>The Automotive mmWave Radar Sensor Market consists of two main types: Short and Mid-range mmWave and Long Range mmWave. Short and Mid-range mmWave sensors are designed to detect objects in close proximity to the vehicle, typically within a range of a few meters. These sensors are commonly used for features like blind-spot detection and parking assistance. On the other hand, Long Range mmWave sensors can detect objects at a greater distance, often up to hundreds of meters, making them suitable for applications such as adaptive cruise control and autonomous driving.</p></p>
