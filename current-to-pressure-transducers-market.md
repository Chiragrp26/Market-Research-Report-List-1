<p><p>Current to pressure transducers, also known as I/P transducers, are devices used to convert an electrical current signal into a proportional pneumatic pressure output. These transducers can be categorized into three market types: DIN Rail, Rack Mount, and Field Mount. DIN Rail transducers are compact and typically utilized in industrial control panel applications. Rack Mount transducers are designed to be integrated into standard 19-inch rack systems and are commonly used in testing and measurement applications. Field Mount transducers are rugged devices suitable for outdoor installations and are used in various industries such as oil and gas, chemical, and water treatment.</p></p>
