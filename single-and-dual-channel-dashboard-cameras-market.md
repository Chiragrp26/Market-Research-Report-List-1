<p><p>Single Channel Dashboard Cameras refer to dashcams that only have one camera lens, typically mounted on the front windshield, capturing the view in front of the vehicle. They are commonly used by individual drivers who want to record their journeys and ensure safety. On the other hand, Dual Channel Dashboard Cameras have an additional camera lens, usually placed on the rear windshield, allowing for simultaneous recording of both the front and rear view of the vehicle. Multi-Channel Dashcams usually have more than two camera lenses, enabling recording from multiple angles, making them suitable for commercial vehicles or larger vehicles.</p></p>
