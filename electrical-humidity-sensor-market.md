<p><ul><li>Capacitive</li><li>Resistive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electrical humidity sensor market is divided into two main types: capacitive and resistive. Capacitive humidity sensors measure humidity by detecting changes in electrical capacitance as moisture levels vary. They are highly accurate and responsive, making them popular for various applications. On the other hand, resistive humidity sensors rely on changes in the electrical resistance of certain materials to determine humidity levels. They are less expensive but provide slightly lower accuracy. Both types have their advantages and are widely utilized in industries such as HVAC, agriculture, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
