<p><p>Military vehicle optronics refers to the use of optical and electronic devices in military vehicles for surveillance, target acquisition, and enhancing situational awareness. The market for military vehicle optronics is divided into two types, namely multispectral and hyperspectral. Multispectral optronics involves the use of various sensors to capture and analyze different wavelength bands. On the other hand, hyperspectral optronics goes a step further by analyzing a much wider range of wavelength bands, allowing for more detailed analysis and identification of targets. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing military capabilities and decision-making.</p></p>
