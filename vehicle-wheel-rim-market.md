<p><p>The vehicle wheel rim market can be categorized into two types: tubeless wheel rims and tube wheel rims. Tubeless wheel rims are designed to accommodate tubeless tires, which do not require an inner tube for inflation. These rims offer improved safety, fuel efficiency, and reduced maintenance costs. On the other hand, tube wheel rims are suitable for tires that require an inner tube. They provide better stability and are commonly used in heavy-duty vehicles such as trucks and buses. Both types of wheel rims cater to different tire requirements and offer unique advantages in the automotive industry.</p></p>
