Amino acids, on the other hand, are essential building blocks in protein synthesis for plants. They enhance nutrient absorption, root growth, and overall plant vigor. Other acid-based biostimulants include seaweed extracts, plant growth regulators, and microbial inoculants, which offer various benefits such as stress tolerance, disease resistance, and improved nutrient availability for plants.</p></p>
