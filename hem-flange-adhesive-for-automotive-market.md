There are two types of hem flange adhesive: one-component and two-component. One-component adhesive is ready to use and doesn't require mixing. It cures when exposed to moisture or heat. Two-component adhesive, on the other hand, requires mixing a base and an activator before application. It offers a faster curing time and higher bond strength but requires precise mixing ratios for optimal performance.</p></p>
