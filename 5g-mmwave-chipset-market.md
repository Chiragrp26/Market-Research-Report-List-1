<p><p>The 5G mmWave Chipset market includes two important types: Baseband Processor and Radio Frequency Integrated Circuit (RFIC). A Baseband Processor is responsible for managing the data transmission between different connected devices and the base station. It handles various tasks like encoding, decoding, and modulating the signals. On the other hand, Radio Frequency Integrated Circuit (RFIC) is responsible for transmitting and receiving signals between the mobile device and the base station at high frequencies, enabling faster data transfer rates and improved network performance in the mmWave spectrum.</p></p>
