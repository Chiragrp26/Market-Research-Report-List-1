<p><p>Chemical film coating is utilized in various industries to enhance the performance and appearance of surfaces. The market can be divided into two main types: primer coating and topcoat coating. Primer coating acts as a foundation layer, ensuring smooth adhesion between the surface and the subsequent coatings, enhancing durability and corrosion resistance. On the other hand, topcoat coating is the final layer applied to provide aesthetic properties such as color, gloss, and protection against weathering. Both types of coatings play crucial roles in improving the properties and lifespan of coated surfaces.</p></p>
