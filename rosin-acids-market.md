<p><p>Rosin acids, also known as rosin-based acids or tall oil rosin acids (TORAs), are a group of organic compounds derived from the distillation of crude tall oil, a byproduct of the kraft pulping process in the paper industry. Rosin acids mainly consist of abietic acid, pimaric acid, and isopimaric acid. They are widely used in various industries such as adhesives, rubber, coatings, inks, paper sizing, and soaps.</p><p>The global rosin acids market is expected to experience significant growth during the forecast period.
