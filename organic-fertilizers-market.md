<p><p>The organic fertilizers market can be categorized into three types based on their sources: plant, animal, and mineral. Plant-based organic fertilizers are derived from plant materials such as compost, green manure, and seaweed. Animal-based organic fertilizers are made from animal byproducts like manure, bone meal, and fish emulsion. Mineral-based organic fertilizers are derived from naturally occurring minerals like rock phosphate and potassium sulfate. These different types of organic fertilizers provide essential nutrients to plants and improve soil fertility in an environmentally friendly manner.</p></p>
