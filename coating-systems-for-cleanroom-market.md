<p><p>Coating systems for cleanroom markets can be categorized into three types: semi-gloss color, matt paint, and light paint. Semi-gloss color is a type of coating that provides a subtle shine and is easy to clean, making it suitable for environments requiring frequent cleaning. Matt paint, on the other hand, has a non-reflective smooth finish, minimizing the risk of glare and reflection. Lastly, light paint is used to enhance brightness in a cleanroom by reflecting more light, ensuring a well-illuminated environment. These different coating systems cater to various cleanroom requirements and aesthetics.</p></p>
