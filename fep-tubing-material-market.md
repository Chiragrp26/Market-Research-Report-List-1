On the other hand, Extruded Tubing is manufactured by extruding the FEP material through a die, resulting in a continuous length of tubing. It is utilized in various industries such as chemicals, pharmaceuticals, and electrical equipment due to its flexibility and resistance to heat and chemicals.</p></p>
