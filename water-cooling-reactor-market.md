<p><strong>The Water Cooling Reactor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Plate Water Cooling Reactor</li><li>Tube Water Cooling Reactor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The water cooling reactor market is categorized into two types: plate water cooling reactors and tube water cooling reactors. Plate water cooling reactors utilize a plate design to transfer heat from the reactor to the cooling water. This design allows for efficient heat transfer due to the large surface area of the plates. On the other hand, tube water cooling reactors use a tube design to circulate cooling water around the reactor, absorbing heat in the process. This design is commonly used in nuclear power plants and industrial settings to cool reactors effectively.</p></p>
