<p><p>Polypropylene Industrial Tapes are widely used in various industries for bonding and sealing applications. The market can be classified into two types: Single-sided Tape and Double-sided Tape. Single-sided Tape is coated with adhesive on one side, making it suitable for applications where only one surface needs to be bonded or sealed. On the other hand, Double-sided Tape has adhesive on both sides, providing enhanced bonding strength for applications requiring the joining of two surfaces. Both types of tapes offer strong adhesion, durability, and resistance to moisture, chemicals, and extreme temperatures.</p></p>
