<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The semiconductor CD SEM market consists of two main types: Photomask CD-SEM and Wafer CD-SEM. Photomask CD-SEM is used for measuring the critical dimensions of photomasks, which are essential for printing patterns on wafers. It ensures the accuracy and quality of the patterns before transferring them to wafers. On the other hand, Wafer CD-SEM is employed for measuring the critical dimensions of patterns directly on the wafers themselves. This helps in maintaining the precision of the patterns during the semiconductor manufacturing process.</p></p>
