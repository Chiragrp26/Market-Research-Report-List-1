Eustachian Tube Balloon Dilation System is a medical device used to treat Eustachian tube dysfunction, a condition that affects the middle ear's ability to equalize pressure. It involves inserting a small balloon into the Eustachian tube and inflating it to open up the narrow passage. Sinus Balloon Dilation System, on the other hand, is used for treating sinusitis. It works similarly by inserting and inflating a balloon in the sinus passageways to expand and clear the obstructed areas.</p></p>
