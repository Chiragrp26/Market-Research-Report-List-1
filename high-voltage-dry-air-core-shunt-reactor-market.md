<p><p>The High Voltage Dry Air Core Shunt Reactor market comprises two main types: Simplex and Three Phase. Simplex reactors consist of a single coil wound around a magnetic core, primarily used in small-scale applications. On the other hand, Three Phase reactors are composed of three individual coils wound around a shared magnetic core, typically employed in larger-scale power systems. Both types of reactors play a crucial role in regulating voltage levels and ensuring stable electricity transmission across the grid.</p></p>
