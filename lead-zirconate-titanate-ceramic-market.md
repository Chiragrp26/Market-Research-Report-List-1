<p><p>Lead Zirconate Titanate (PZT) ceramic is a ferroelectric material that exhibits piezoelectric properties, meaning it can convert mechanical stress into electrical signals and vice versa. One of the key drivers for this growth is the increasing demand for piezoelectric ceramic materials in the electronics and automotive industries. PZT ceramics are extensively used in sensors for automotive applications, such as airbag deployment, fuel injection control, and engine management. The growing automotive industry across the globe is thus driving the demand for PZT ceramics.</p><p>Moreover, the rising adoption of internet of things (IoT) devices is also contributing to the market growth. PZT ceramics are used in various IoT sensors for applications like healthcare monitoring, industrial automation, and smart homes. As the IoT market continues to expand, the demand for PZT ceramics is expected to increase further.</p><p>In addition, the increasing focus on energy harvesting technologies is driving the demand for PZT ceramics.
