There are two main types of EGT sensors on the market: Positive Temperature Coefficient (PTC) and Negative Temperature Coefficient (NTC) sensors. PTC sensors exhibit an increase in electrical resistance with rising temperature, while NTC sensors display a decrease in resistance. Both sensor types provide crucial data to the engine control unit, aiding in the optimization of engine performance, emission control, and ensuring the safety of the vehicle.</p></p>
