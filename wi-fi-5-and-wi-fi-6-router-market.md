On the other hand, Wi-Fi 6 routers conform to the IEEE 802.11ax standard and operate on both 2.4 GHz and 5 GHz bands. They provide faster speeds of up to 9.6 Gbps, improved connection stability, lower latency, and can handle more connected devices simultaneously, making them ideal for larger homes and crowded environments.</p></p>
