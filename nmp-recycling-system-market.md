<p><p>The NMP (N-Methyl-2-pyrrolidone) Recycling System market encompasses two different market types: Freeze Recovery and Wheel Recovery. The Freeze Recovery market focuses on the recycling of NMP by freezing it to remove impurities and separate the solvent, allowing it to be reused. On the other hand, the Wheel Recovery market involves the regeneration of NMP through a process called wheel evaporation, which uses high temperatures and advanced technology to recover and purify the solvent efficiently. Both market types aim to minimize waste and promote the sustainable reuse of NMP in various industries.</p></p>
