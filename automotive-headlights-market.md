<p><p>The automotive headlights market is categorized into various types. Xenon headlights use xenon gas to produce a bright, high-intensity light. HID headlights, also known as high-intensity discharge headlights, rely on an electrical charge to light up a combination of gases and metallic salts. LED headlights use light-emitting diodes to produce light, offering energy efficiency and long life. Laser headlights produce an intense and focused beam using laser diodes. The "Others" category encompasses headlights that use different technologies or a combination of the above, including halogen and adaptive headlights.</p></p>
