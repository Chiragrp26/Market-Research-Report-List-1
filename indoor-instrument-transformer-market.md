<p><p>The indoor instrument transformer market involves the types of current transformers and potential transformers. Current transformers are used to step down high currents to lower values that can be safely measured or monitored. They are commonly used in power distribution systems to protect and monitor electrical equipment. On the other hand, potential transformers are used to step down high voltages to a level that can be safely measured. They are commonly used in voltage measurement applications such as metering and monitoring in power systems. Both current and potential transformers play crucial roles in ensuring the safety and effectiveness of indoor electrical installations.</p></p>
