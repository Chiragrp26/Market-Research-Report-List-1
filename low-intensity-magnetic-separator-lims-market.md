<p><p>The Low Intensity Magnetic Separator (LIMS) market includes two main types: the Dry Low Intensity Magnetic Separator (LIMS) and the Wet Low Intensity Magnetic Separator (LIMS). The dry LIMS uses a rotating magnetic drum to separate magnetic particles from non-magnetic ones without the need for water. On the other hand, the wet LIMS utilizes water to facilitate the separation process and is typically used in industries where water is readily available. Both types are essential in various industries, including mining and recycling, for the efficient extraction of magnetic materials.</p></p>
