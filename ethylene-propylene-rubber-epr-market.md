<p><p>Ethylene Propylene Rubber (EPR) is a type of synthetic rubber that is commonly used in various industries. There are two main types of EPR: Ethylene Propylene Monomer (EPM) and Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer (EPDM). EPM rubber is primarily used for manufacturing seals, gaskets, and hoses due to its excellent resistance to heat, ozone, and chemicals. EPDM rubber, on the other hand, is more versatile and finds application in automotive parts, construction materials, and electrical insulation due to its superior durability and weather resistance.</p></p>
