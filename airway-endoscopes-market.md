<p><p>Airway endoscopes are instrumental in the diagnosis and treatment of various respiratory conditions. The market for airway endoscopes can be classified into two types: flexible endoscopes and rigid endoscopes. Flexible endoscopes are characterized by their ability to bend and navigate through the complex airway passages, providing detailed visualizations. On the other hand, rigid endoscopes have a straight structure and are primarily used for procedures requiring rigid access to the airway. Both types play a critical role in facilitating effective treatment and management of respiratory diseases.</p></p>
