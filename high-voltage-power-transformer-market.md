<p><p>The high-voltage power transformer market includes two main types: three-phase transformers and single-phase transformers. Three-phase transformers are designed to transfer high voltage electrical power through three separate circuits, making them suitable for large-scale industrial applications. In contrast, single-phase transformers transfer power through a single circuit, generally used for smaller residential or commercial purposes. Both types play a crucial role in supplying electrical power efficiently and safely, albeit with different capacities and scale of operations.</p></p>
