<p><p>The automotive pressure switch market includes various types of switches used in vehicles. Dual function pressure switches are versatile switches that can control both the high and low-pressure conditions. High-Pressure Cut-Out (HPCO) switches protect the system from excessive pressure by shutting it off. Fan pressure switches control the activation of the cooling fan based on pressure levels. Compressor cycling switches regulate the operation of the compressor to prevent damage or overheating. These different types of switches cater to specific needs in the automotive industry, ensuring safety and optimal performance.</p></p>
