Cobalt targets offer good magnetic properties, making them ideal for magnetic thin film deposition. Nickel targets are used for corrosion-resistant coatings, and Titanium targets are chosen for their high strength-to-weight ratio. Other materials may include alloys and compounds tailored for specific applications.</p></p>
