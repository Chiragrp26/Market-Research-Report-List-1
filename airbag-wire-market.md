<p><p>The Airbag Wire market consists of two main types: OEM and Aftermarket. OEM refers to Original Equipment Manufacturer, where the wire is provided by the vehicle manufacturer themselves. These wires are specifically designed and manufactured to meet the specifications of the vehicle. On the other hand, Aftermarket refers to wires that are produced by third-party manufacturers and are not original to the vehicle. These wires are generally more cost-effective and easily available, but may not necessarily meet the exact specifications as OEM wires.</p></p>
