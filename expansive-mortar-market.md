<p><p>The expansive mortar market is categorized into three types based on the temperature conditions for its application on rock (reinforced concrete) structures. Each type corresponds to a specific temperature range. Rock (Reinforced Concrete)-I is suitable for temperatures ranging from 25℃ to 35℃, Rock (Reinforced Concrete)-II is recommended for temperatures between 15℃ and 25℃, and Rock (Reinforced Concrete)-III is designed for temperatures ranging from 0℃ to 15℃. These temperature-specific types ensure optimal performance and effectiveness of expansive mortar in the demolition or splitting of rock (reinforced concrete) structures.</p></p>
