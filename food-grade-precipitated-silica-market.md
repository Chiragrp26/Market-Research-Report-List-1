Firstly, with the increasing demand for processed and convenience foods, the need for food additives, such as precipitated silica, is also rising. Precipitated silica helps in maintaining the quality and shelf life of these food products. Secondly, the growing consumer awareness regarding food safety and the need for natural and clean label ingredients is driving the demand for food grade precipitated silica. It is considered safe for consumption and does not have any adverse health effects. It aids in enhancing the texture, stability, and flow properties of the food item.
