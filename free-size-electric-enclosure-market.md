Wall-mounted enclosures are fixed on walls and are suitable for spaces with limited area. Floor-mounted or free-standing enclosures are placed on the floor and offer more space for equipment and cables. Underground enclosures are buried beneath the ground and protect electrical components in areas where above-ground installation is not feasible. These enclosure types cater to different needs and ensure the safety and organization of electric equipment.</p></p>
