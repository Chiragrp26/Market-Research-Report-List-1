<p><p>Hot box resin for casting is a type of resin used in foundry applications that requires heat curing. There are three main types of hot box resins: phenolic resin, furan resin (also known as furfuryl alcohol resin), and urea formaldehyde resin. </p><p>Phenolic resin is widely used due to its excellent heat resistance and dimensional stability. Furan resin offers good collapsibility and low gas emissions during casting. Urea formaldehyde resin is known for its low toxicity and cost-effectiveness. These resins play a crucial role in the manufacturing of high-quality casting products for various industries.</p></p>
