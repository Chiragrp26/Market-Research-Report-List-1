<p><p>Independent Water and Power Producer (IWPP) market refers to a setup where private companies are responsible for building, owning, and operating desalination plants to produce water and energy. Multi-effect Distillation and Multi-stage Flash Desalination are two types of technologies used in this market. Multi-effect Distillation involves heating seawater in different chambers to produce steam, which is then condensed to obtain freshwater. Multi-stage Flash Desalination, on the other hand, involves flashing seawater at different pressure levels to produce freshwater by condensing the vapor. Both these technologies play a significant role in meeting the growing water demand in various regions.</p></p>
