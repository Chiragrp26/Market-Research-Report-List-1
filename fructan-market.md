<p><strong>The Fructan Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inulin Type</li><li>Left Glycan Type</li><li>Mixed Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fructan market can be categorized into three types: Inulin type, Left Glycan type, and Mixed type. Inulin type fructans are composed primarily of long chains of fructose and have a high degree of polymerization. Left Glycan type fructans consist of both fructose and glucose molecules and have a lower degree of polymerization compared to inulin type. Mixed type fructans are a combination of both inulin type and left glycan type fructans. These different types cater to diverse consumer preferences and industry applications.</p></p>
