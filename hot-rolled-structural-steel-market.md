<p><p>Hot rolled structural steel market consists of two main types: carbon structure steel and alloy structure steel. Carbon structure steel is primarily made of iron and carbon and is characterized by its high tensile strength and low cost. It is widely used in construction and manufacturing industries for its durability. On the other hand, alloy structure steel contains additional elements like manganese, chromium, and nickel, which enhance its mechanical properties such as strength, hardness, and corrosion resistance. This type of steel finds applications in automotive, aerospace, and machinery industries.</p></p>
