<p><p>The in vitro organ preservation market involves various techniques to maintain organs outside the human body before transplantation. Static cold storage is the traditional method where organs are cooled to low temperatures using a solution. Hypothermic machine perfusion uses a machine to circulate a cold solution through the organ, providing oxygen and nutrients. Normothermic machine perfusion is a newer method that maintains the organ at normal body temperature, keeping it functional and reducing damage. These techniques play a crucial role in preserving organs and improving transplant outcomes.</p></p>
