<p><p>The motion sensor market for wearables can be classified into two types: accelerometer and gyroscope. An accelerometer measures acceleration forces, allowing it to track movements such as walking or jumping. It detects changes in velocity and calculates various motion characteristics. On the other hand, a gyroscope measures angular velocity, enabling it to detect rotational movements like tilting or rotating. It provides data on orientation changes and can be used in conjunction with an accelerometer to provide more accurate motion tracking capabilities for wearable devices.</p></p>
