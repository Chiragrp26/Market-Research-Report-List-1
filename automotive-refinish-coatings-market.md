<p><p>The automotive refinish coatings market encompasses various types of coatings used for refinishing vehicles. The most commonly used types are water-borne, solvent-borne, and UV-cured coatings. Water-borne coatings are environmentally friendly, as they contain low levels of VOCs. Solvent-borne coatings use organic solvents as a carrier, which can have a negative impact on the environment. UV-cured coatings, on the other hand, are cured using ultraviolet light, providing a fast-drying and durable finish. The market also differentiates coatings based on resin types, which include alkyd, acrylic, polyurethane, and epoxy coatings. Each resin type offers different properties and benefits for automotive refinishing applications.</p></p>

<p><strong>What are the Emerging Trends in the Global Automotive Refinish Coatings market?</strong></p>
<p><p>The global automotive refinish coatings market is witnessing several emerging trends. One of them is the increasing demand for eco-friendly paints, driven by environmental concerns and stricter regulations.
