<p><p>Copper bars, also known as copper rods, are available in different market types. EC (Electrolytic Tough Pitch) copper bars have high electrical conductivity and are used in electrical applications. ETP (Electrolytic Tough Pitch) copper bars have lower impurity content and are commonly used in plumbing and electrical systems. DHP (Deoxidized High Phosphorus) copper bars have improved corrosion resistance and are used in plumbing applications. Oxygen Free copper bars have very low oxygen content, making them suitable for high-performance electrical components. Silver Bearing copper bars contain small amounts of silver to enhance conductivity. Other market types include specialized copper bars tailored for specific applications.</p></p>
