<p><p>Color brightness meters are devices used to measure the brightness of a color. There are two types available in the market. The first type uses three sensors (red, green, blue) to measure the color of an object by capturing the intensity of each color channel. This information is then used to calculate the overall color brightness. The second type focuses on measuring the spectral distribution of a color, which refers to the distribution of wavelengths in the color spectrum. This data helps in analyzing the color quality and consistency.</p></p>
