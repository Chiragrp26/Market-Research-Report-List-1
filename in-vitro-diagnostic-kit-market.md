<p><p>The In Vitro Diagnostic (IVD) Kit market can be categorized into several types. Immuno Diagnostics involves the detection of antibodies or antigens in body fluids to diagnose diseases or monitor conditions. Chemistry Diagnostics focuses on measuring various substances in body fluids to assess organ function or detect abnormalities. Molecular Diagnostics analyzes genetic material to identify genetic disorders or infectious diseases. Point-of-Care Testing (POCT) refers to portable and rapid diagnostic tests used at the patient's location. Lastly, there are other markets within IVD, such as hematology, microbiology, and urinalysis, which encompass specific diagnostic areas.</p></p>
