<p><strong>The Radiation Detection Material Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Semiconductors</li><li>Scintillators</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Radiation detection materials encompass various types such as semiconductors, scintillators, and others. Semiconductors are typically made of silicon or germanium, and when exposed to radiation, they produce electrical signals proportional to the detected radiation. Scintillators, on the other hand, consist of crystalline materials like sodium iodide or cesium iodide that emit light when radiation interacts with them. These materials can be used to convert radiation into detectable signals. Lastly, the category "others" refers to diverse materials such as thermoluminescent detectors or gaseous detectors, which are used in specific applications based on their unique properties.</p></p>
