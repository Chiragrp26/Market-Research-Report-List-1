<p><p>The mutual inductor market comprises three main types: electromagnetic, capacitive, and optoelectronic. Electromagnetic mutual inductors use the principle of electromagnetic induction to generate an electrical current in a coil. Capacitive mutual inductors utilize the capacitance between two conductive plates to store and release energy. Optoelectronic mutual inductors employ light signals and optical fibers to transmit and receive information. These market types cater to various applications such as power generation, communication systems, and electronic devices, each offering different advantages and functionalities.</p></p>
