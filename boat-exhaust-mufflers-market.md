<p><ul><li>Chambered Exhaust Muffler</li><li>Straight Through Exhaust Muffler</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The boat exhaust mufflers market consists of three main types: chambered exhaust mufflers, straight-through exhaust mufflers, and others. Chambered exhaust mufflers are designed with internal chambers that create a series of sound-canceling waves, reducing noise levels. Straight-through exhaust mufflers, on the other hand, feature a straight-through design that allows exhaust gases to flow straight through the muffler, thereby minimizing exhaust backpressure and increasing engine performance. The "others" category includes various designs that may combine features of both chambered and straight-through mufflers or have unique characteristics.</p></p>
