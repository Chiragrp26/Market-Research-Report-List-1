<p><p>The Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition (SCADA) market can be categorized into three types: hardware, software, and services. </p><p>The hardware segment includes devices that form the foundation of a SCADA system, such as remote terminal units (RTUs) and programmable logic controllers (PLCs). These devices collect and process data from various sensors and send it to the central control system.</p><p>The software segment includes the software applications that enable users to monitor, control, and analyze the collected data. This includes the SCADA software itself, as well as other related applications like human-machine interface (HMI) software.</p><p>The services segment includes various services offered by SCADA system providers, such as installation, integration, maintenance, and support. These services ensure the smooth functioning and reliability of the SCADA system for the end-users.</p></p>
