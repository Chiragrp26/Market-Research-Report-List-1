<p><p>MEMS Fiber Optic Switches are devices used to connect or disconnect optical fibers in telecommunication networks. There are two types of switches: MEMS Single-mode Optical Switches and MEMS Multimode Optical Switches. Single-mode switches are designed for applications that demand high precision and low insertion loss, commonly used in long-distance transmission systems. On the other hand, multimode switches are used in short-range communication networks, where cost-effectiveness and simplicity are prioritized. These switches play a critical role in ensuring efficient and reliable fiber optic communication.</p></p>
