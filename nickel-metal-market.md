<p><p>Nickeliferous limonite smelting refers to the process of extracting nickel from limonite ore, which contains a significant amount of nickel. This type of smelting involves heating the limonite ore at high temperatures to melt the nickel and separate it from other impurities. On the other hand, garnierite smelting involves the extraction of nickel from garnierite ore, which is rich in nickel and magnesium. This process also involves high-temperature heating to remove impurities and obtain pure nickel. Both types of smelting are crucial in the nickel metal market for producing this valuable metal.</p></p>
