<p><strong>The Nickel Matte Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Layered Smelting</li><li>Leaching</li><li>Carbonyl Process</li><li>Flotation Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Nickel Matte market consists of four major market types: Layered Smelting, Leaching, Carbonyl Process, and Flotation Process. Layered Smelting involves the extraction of nickel from sulfide ores through the process of smelting. Leaching involves the dissolution of nickel from low-grade laterites using acid or ammonia. The Carbonyl Process utilizes high-pressure carbon monoxide to extract pure nickel from nickel metal powders. The Flotation Process involves the separation of nickel sulfide minerals from other sulfide minerals through a froth flotation process. Each market type caters to different nickel extraction methods and has its own distinct demand and pricing factors.</p></p>
