<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Cloud video conferencing solutions can be categorized into two types: Scalable Video Coding (SVC) and Advanced Video Coding (AVC). SVC type allows for efficient transmission of video by dividing it into multiple layers, enabling receivers to selectively decode layers based on their available bandwidth. This ensures smooth video communication even in low bandwidth situations. On the other hand, AVC type utilizes more traditional video coding techniques. Both types cater to different needs, providing flexible options for users in the cloud video conferencing market.</p></p>
