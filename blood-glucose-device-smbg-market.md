<p><p>The Blood Glucose Device (SMBG) market includes three main components: strips, glucose meters, and lancets. Strips are small disposable test strips that are inserted into the glucose meter to measure blood glucose levels. Glucose meters are portable devices that analyze the blood sample obtained from the strips to provide accurate readings. Lancets are small needles used to prick the finger and draw a blood sample for testing. These three components work together to provide individuals with a convenient and efficient way to monitor their blood glucose levels at home.</p></p>
