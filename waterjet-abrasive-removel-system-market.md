<p><p>A waterjet abrasive removal system is used to efficiently remove spent abrasive materials from a waterjet cutting machine. Waterjet cutting is a process that utilizes a high-pressure jet of water mixed with an abrasive material to cut through various materials including metal, glass, stone, and composites.</p><p>The waterjet abrasive removal system consists of a hopper, where the spent abrasive material is collected, and a separation unit that separates the abrasive material from the water. This allows for the efficient recycling and reuse of the abrasive material, reducing costs and environmental impact.</p><p>The waterjet abrasive removal system market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. The increasing adoption of waterjet cutting machines across various industries, such as automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing, is a major factor driving the market growth.
