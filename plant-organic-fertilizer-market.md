<p><p>The plant organic fertilizer market can be categorized into two types: naturally prepared and chemical synthesis. Naturally prepared organic fertilizers are made from natural sources such as animal manure, compost, and plant residues. These materials are biodegradable and improve soil structure and nutrient content. On the other hand, chemical synthesis organic fertilizers are manufactured through chemical processes, using mineral resources. These fertilizers are often highly concentrated and provide specific nutrient ratios to suit different plant requirements. Both types of fertilizers play a vital role in enhancing plant growth and crop yields.</p></p>
