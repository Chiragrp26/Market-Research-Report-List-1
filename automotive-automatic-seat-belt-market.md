<p><p>The automotive automatic seat belt market can be categorized into three types: two-point, three-point, and above four-point. The two-point type includes a lap belt that goes over the pelvis, while the three-point type adds a shoulder belt that crosses the chest. The above four-point type offers additional points of attachment, providing extra security and stability by restraining the occupant at more than just the pelvis and chest. These different types aim to enhance passenger safety and compliance with seat belt regulations in automobiles.</p></p>
