<p><p>The Tablet & Notebook Display market offers three types of display technologies. LCD (Liquid Crystal Display) is the most common type, known for its energy efficiency and vibrant colors. OLED (Organic Light-Emitting Diode) display provides superior picture quality with its self-emitting pixels, resulting in high contrast and wide viewing angles. AMOLED (Active-Matrix Organic Light-Emitting Diode) display offers similar benefits to OLED, but with an active-matrix design for better performance and higher resolution. These display options cater to different user preferences, providing varied visual experiences for tablets and notebooks.</p></p>
