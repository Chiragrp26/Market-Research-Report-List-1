<p><strong>The Automotive Cam Phaser Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vane-type Cam Phaser</li><li>Star Shaped Cam Phaser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive cam phaser market consists of two main types, namely vane-type cam phasers and star-shaped cam phasers. Vane-type cam phasers utilize vanes that rotate within a phaser housing to control the position of the camshaft relative to the crankshaft. On the other hand, star-shaped cam phasers employ a star-shaped rotor with multiple lobes that engage with the phaser housing to adjust the camshaft's timing. Both types play a crucial role in optimizing engine performance by enabling variable valve timing, enhancing fuel efficiency, and improving overall power output.</p></p>
