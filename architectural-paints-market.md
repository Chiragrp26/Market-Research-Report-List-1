<p><p>Architectural paints market is categorized into several types. Water-soluble paints are formulated with water as the main solvent, making them eco-friendly and easy to clean. Solvent paints, on the other hand, use organic solvents for suspension and drying. Emulsion paints contain water as well as polymer binders, offering good adhesion and durability. Powder paints consist of finely ground particles that are electrostatically applied and then heated to form a tough coating. Lastly, there are other types of paints with unique characteristics, such as anti-microbial or heat-resistant properties.</p></p>
