<p><p>Hydrophilic fumed silica is a synthetic, amorphous substance made from the oxidation of silicon tetrachloride in a flame of hydrogen and oxygen. It is a nanoparticle material with a high surface area, typically around 200 m2/g, and exhibits excellent hydrophilic properties due to the presence of silanol groups on its surface. It is commonly used as a thickening, reinforcing, and anticaking agent in various industries including paints & coatings, adhesives & sealants, cosmetics, pharmaceuticals, and others.</p><p>The hydrophilic fumed silica market is witnessing significant growth due to its versatile characteristics and wide-ranging applications. The increasing demand from industries such as paints & coatings, adhesives & sealants, and personal care is driving the market growth. Hydrophilic fumed silica is extensively used as a rheology modifier in water-based systems and as a thixotrope for improving anti-settling properties and viscosity control in various formulations. Additionally, its excellent reinforcement properties make it a preferred choice in rubber and silicone rubber applications.</p><p>Moreover, the growing awareness and focus on sustainability and eco-friendly products are further driving the demand for hydrophilic fumed silica. It acts as a green replacement for conventional thickeners and exhibits minimal impact on the environment. It also provides improved properties to manufactured products, such as enhanced dispersibility, transparency, and storage stability.</p><p>In terms of regional analysis, Asia-Pacific is expected to dominate the hydrophilic fumed silica market during the forecast period.
