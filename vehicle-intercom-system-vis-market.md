<p><p>The Vehicle Intercom System (VIS) market encompasses two main types: Digital Vehicular Intercom and Analog Vehicular Intercom. </p><p>Digital Vehicular Intercom systems utilize advanced digital technology to enhance communication in vehicles. These systems offer superior sound quality, noise cancellation, encryption, and are easy to integrate with other devices. They provide reliable and secure communication for military, emergency services, and commercial vehicle applications.</p><p>On the other hand, Analog Vehicular Intercom systems use analog signals for communication. These systems are cost-effective and have been widely used in various vehicle applications before the rise of digital technology. However, compared to digital systems, analog intercoms may have limitations in sound quality and security measures.</p></p>
