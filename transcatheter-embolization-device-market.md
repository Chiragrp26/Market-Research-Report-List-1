<p><p>The Transcatheter Embolization Device Market includes three types: Particle embolic, Coil embolic, and Liquid embolic. Particle embolic devices, made of small beads or microspheres, are used to block the blood vessels supplying abnormal tissues or tumors. Coil embolic devices, made of platinum or other metals, are used to block the blood flow by creating a coil-like structure. Liquid embolic devices, made of liquid embolic agents, are injected into blood vessels to block and solidify them. These different types of devices provide options for interventional radiologists to effectively treat various vascular conditions.</p></p>
