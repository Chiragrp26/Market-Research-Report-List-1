<p><p>Fiber optic power meters are categorized into Type I and Type II markets. Type I typically includes compact portable power meters suitable for field testing. They are designed to measure both average power and power loss with accuracy and reliability. Type II, on the other hand, usually refers to higher-end power meters used for laboratory or specialized applications. These meters provide advanced features like multiple wavelength measurements, high power handling capacity, and greater measurement accuracy. Both Type I and Type II power meters cater to different needs and applications within the fiber optic industry.</p></p>
