<p><p>Vehicle gear reducers are vital components in automobiles that help transmit power from the engine to the wheels. There are various types of gear reducers available in the market. Automatic Transmission (AT) gear reducers are commonly used in traditional automatic transmissions, providing smooth gear shifting. Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT) gear reducers offer seamless acceleration by enabling an infinite number of gear ratios. Dual Clutch Transmission (DCT) gear reducers combine the efficiency of manual and automatic transmissions, ensuring rapid gear changes. The "Others" category includes various gear reducers, such as manual transmissions, sequential transmissions, and hybrid transmissions, offering different functionalities for diverse vehicle models.</p></p>
