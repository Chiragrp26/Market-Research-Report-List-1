<p><p>The recyclable thermosets market encompasses three types of recycling methods: mechanical recycling, energy recycling, and feedstock recycling. In mechanical recycling, thermoset products are broken down into smaller pieces, which are then used to create new products. Energy recycling involves the burning of thermoset waste to generate heat or electricity. Lastly, feedstock recycling involves converting thermoset waste back into its original chemical structure to be used as raw materials for new products. These methods offer sustainable solutions for managing and reusing thermoset materials, reducing waste and promoting a circular economy.</p></p>
