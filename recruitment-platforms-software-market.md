<p><p>Recruitment platform software refers to technology solutions designed for managing the hiring process. There are two types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based software is hosted on the internet and accessed through web browsers. It offers flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness as data is stored and processed in the cloud.
