<p><p>The Photoluminescence (PL) Mapper market includes two types: Room Temperature PL Mapper and Low Temperature PL Mapper. Room Temperature PL Mappers operate at ambient conditions and are capable of mapping the photoluminescent properties of materials without the need for cooling. Low Temperature PL Mappers, on the other hand, require cooling to low temperatures to study the photoluminescent properties of materials that only exhibit luminescence at low temperatures. Both types of PL Mappers are used for research and analysis purposes in various industries such as electronics, materials science, and photonics.</p></p>
