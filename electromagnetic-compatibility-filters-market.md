<p><strong>The Electromagnetic Compatibility Filters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Type I electromagnetic compatibility (EMC) filters are designed to reduce conducted emissions from electronic devices to meet regulatory standards. They focus on reducing conducted noise that is generated by the device itself. Type I filters are commonly used in consumer electronics and small appliances.</p><p>Type II EMC filters are more advanced, providing both conducted emissions reduction and conducted susceptibility protection. They are designed to minimize electromagnetic interference between multiple devices within a system. Type II filters are typically used in industrial applications, where multiple electronic devices coexist and interact.</p></p>
