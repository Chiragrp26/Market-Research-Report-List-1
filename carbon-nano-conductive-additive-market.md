Carbon Nanofibers possess high electrical conductivity and are used in aerospace, energy storage, and electronics. Carbon Nanotubes exhibit superior electrical and thermal conductivity and find applications in electronics, automotive, and energy storage. Graphene, a single layer of carbon atoms, is highly conductive and has potential applications in electronics, energy, and composites.</p></p>
