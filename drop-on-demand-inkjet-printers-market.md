<p><p>Drop-on-demand inkjet printers are used for printing purposes and come in three main types: thermal inkjet printers, piezo inkjet printers, and electrostatic inkjet printers. </p><p>Thermal inkjet printers work by using heat to create bubbles within the ink, which are then expelled onto the paper. </p><p>Piezo inkjet printers, on the other hand, use electrical pulses to change the shape of a piezo crystal, thus expelling ink drops onto the paper. </p><p>Lastly, electrostatic inkjet printers use electrostatic forces to propel charged ink drops onto the paper. These different types of printers offer various benefits and features, catering to different printing needs.</p></p>
