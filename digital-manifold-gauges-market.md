<p><p>Digital manifold gauges are versatile tools used in HVACR systems to measure and monitor the pressure of refrigerants. They are available in two market types: high pressure gauge and low pressure gauge. High pressure gauges are designed to measure the pressure of refrigerants in the high-pressure side of the system, typically for systems operating with high-pressure refrigerants. On the other hand, low pressure gauges are used to measure the pressure of refrigerants in the low-pressure side of the system, commonly for systems operating with low-pressure refrigerants. These gauges ensure accurate pressure readings, helping technicians diagnose and troubleshoot HVACR systems effectively.</p></p>
