<p><p>Inkjet inks are available in three main types: water-based, oil-based, and solvent-based. Water-based inks are commonly used in home and office printers as they are non-toxic and produce high-quality prints. Oil-based inks are preferred for their durability and resistance to water and light, making them suitable for outdoor applications like vehicle graphics and signage. Solvent-based inks contain chemicals that dissolve into a liquid, allowing them to print on a variety of surfaces, including plastics and metals, but they can be harmful to the environment and require proper ventilation during printing.</p></p>
