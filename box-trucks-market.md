<p><ul><li>Single Bridge Box Trucks</li><li>Double Bridge Box Trucks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Box trucks, also known as cube vans or panel trucks, come in various market types. Single bridge box trucks are characterized by a single set of wheels at the rear axle, making them suitable for lighter payloads and shorter distances. These trucks are commonly used for local deliveries or moving purposes. On the other hand, double bridge box trucks feature two sets of wheels at the rear axle, offering enhanced stability and the ability to carry heavier loads over longer distances. They are often utilized in industries such as construction, freight transportation, or logistics.</p></p>
