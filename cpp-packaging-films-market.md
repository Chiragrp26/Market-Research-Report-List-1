<p><p>CPP packaging films market is segmented into various types based on their composition and applications. Metalized CPP films have a thin layer of metal coating for enhancing aesthetics and barrier properties, making them suitable for packaging food, pharmaceuticals, and electronics. Retort CPP films have high heat resistance and puncture resistance, making them ideal for packaging ready-to-eat meals and pet food that undergo sterilization. Other markets include CPP films for lamination, labels, printing, and adhesive tapes, providing a wide range of packaging solutions for different industries.</p></p>
