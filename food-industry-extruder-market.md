<p><strong>The Food Industry Extruder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Screw</li><li>Twin Screw</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The food industry extruder market consists of two types: single screw and twin screw extruders. Single screw extruders are designed with a single rotating screw that helps in pushing the food ingredients through the extruder barrel. They are suitable for processing simple food products with low viscosity. On the other hand, twin screw extruders have two intermeshing screws that provide better mixing, kneading, and shearing of food ingredients. They are more versatile and can handle a wider range of food products, including those with high viscosity and complex formulations.</p></p>
