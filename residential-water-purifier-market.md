<p><p>The residential water purifier market consists of multiple types based on technologies used. RO water purifiers use reverse osmosis to remove impurities through a semipermeable membrane. Gravity-based water purifiers rely on gravitational force to purify water, usually through activated carbon filters. UV water purifiers utilize ultraviolet light to kill bacteria and viruses. These different technologies offer varying levels of filtration and purification, providing consumers with options to choose from based on their specific needs and preferences in ensuring clean and safe drinking water at home.</p></p>
