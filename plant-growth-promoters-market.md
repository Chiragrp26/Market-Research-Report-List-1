<p><p>Plant growth promoters are substances that enhance the growth and development of plants. They can be categorized into different types, including cytokinins, auxins, gibberellins, and ethylene plant growth promoters. Cytokinins stimulate cell division and promote shoot formation. Auxins regulate plant growth, influencing root development and phototropism. Gibberellins promote stem elongation and regulate plant growth and flowering. Ethylene is a gas hormone that controls fruit ripening and influences plant growth and development. These different types of plant growth promoters play essential roles in shaping the growth and productivity of plants.</p></p>
