<p><p>Medical packaging paper market types can be classified into coated paper and uncoated paper. Coated paper is a type of paper that has been treated with a coating material, such as clay or latex, which enhances its smoothness, durability, and printability. It offers a higher level of protection against moisture and contaminants, making it suitable for applications where hygiene and sterility are crucial in medical packaging. On the other hand, uncoated paper does not have any added coating, providing a more natural and rough texture. It is commonly used for less sensitive medical packaging needs.</p></p>
