<p><p>Bentonite powder is available in two main types, namely Sodium Bentonite and Calcium Bentonite. Sodium Bentonite has a high swelling capacity and is widely used in industries like foundry, drilling, and waterproofing. It acts as a binder and helps in forming well-structured materials. On the other hand, Calcium Bentonite has excellent absorption properties and is commonly used in industries like cosmetics, medicine, and wastewater treatment. It is known for its detoxifying effects and ability to absorb harmful substances. Both types of Bentonite powder have diverse applications and contribute to various industrial sectors.</p></p>
