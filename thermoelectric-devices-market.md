<p><p>The thermoelectric devices market can be categorized into three main types: Bulk Thermoelectric Devices, Micro Thermoelectric Devices, and Thin film Thermoelectric Devices. Bulk Thermoelectric Devices are larger in size and are commonly used in power generation applications. Micro Thermoelectric Devices are smaller in size and are often used for sensors or cooling applications in electronic devices. Thin film Thermoelectric Devices are made by depositing thin layers of thermoelectric materials onto a substrate and are used in various applications including energy harvesting and waste heat recovery.</p></p>
