<p><p>Optical Clear EMC (Electromagnetic Compatibility) refers to materials used to prevent electromagnetic interference (EMI) in electronic devices. There are two types of Optical Clear EMC materials: Solid Optical Clear EMC and Liquid Optical Clear EMC. The Solid type is a solid-state material, often in the form of film or sheet, providing excellent EMI shielding and transparency. Meanwhile, the Liquid type refers to a solution or coating that can be applied to electronic components, offering EMI protection while maintaining optical clarity. Both types serve to mitigate EMI and ensure the reliable operation of electronic devices.</p></p>
