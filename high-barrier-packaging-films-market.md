<p><p>High barrier packaging films are specifically designed to provide superior protection to the packaged contents from external factors such as moisture, oxygen, and sunlight. PET (polyethylene terephthalate), CPP (cast polypropylene), BOPP (biaxially oriented polypropylene), PVA (polyvinyl alcohol), PLA (polylactic acid), and other materials are commonly used in the market. PET films offer excellent moisture and gas barrier properties, while CPP films provide good flexibility. BOPP films are known for their high tensile strength. PVA films offer excellent gas and aroma barrier, and PLA films are biodegradable. Other materials can include aluminum, ethylene vinyl alcohol copolymer, and nylon.</p></p>
