<p><p>Biomethanol market can be classified into two types: by-product sourced and waste sourced. It is a more sustainable alternative to traditional methanol production. On the other hand, waste sourced biomethanol is derived from organic waste materials, such as agricultural residues or municipal solid waste, through a process called biomass gasification. This type of biomethanol helps in reducing waste and provides an eco-friendly solution for energy production.</p></p>
