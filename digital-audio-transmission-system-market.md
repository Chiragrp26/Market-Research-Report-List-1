<p><p>The digital audio transmission system market can be categorized into two types: wireless-based and wired-based. Wireless-based systems transmit audio signals without the need for physical cables, using technologies such as Bluetooth or Wi-Fi. These systems provide flexibility and mobility but may have limitations in terms of range and potential interference. On the other hand, wired-based systems rely on physical cables to transmit audio signals, ensuring a stable and secure connection. While they may have limited mobility, they generally offer higher fidelity and robustness.</p></p>
