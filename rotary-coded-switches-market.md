<p><strong>The Rotary Coded Switches Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SMT Type</li><li>Through Hole Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Rotary Coded Switches market is segmented into two types: SMT Type and Through Hole Type. SMT (Surface Mount Technology) Type rotary switches are designed to be mounted directly onto the surface of PCBs, offering compact and space-saving benefits. Through Hole Type rotary switches, on the other hand, are mounted using traditional through-hole soldering techniques. They provide a strong and reliable connection to the PCB, making them suitable for applications with higher mechanical stress or environmental conditions. Both types offer different installation options to cater to diverse customer requirements in the rotary coded switches market.</p></p>
