<p><p>Bullet-proof glass is a specialized type of glass used for security purposes. It comes in two market types: unidirectional and two-way. Unidirectional bullet-proof glass is designed to withstand bullets from one direction only, providing protection against attacks from a specific angle. On the other hand, two-way bullet-proof glass offers protection from bullets coming from both sides, ensuring security in situations where attacks can occur from multiple directions. These market types cater to different security needs and are chosen based on specific threats and requirements.</p></p>
