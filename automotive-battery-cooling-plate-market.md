<p><p>The automotive battery cooling plate market consists of three main types - harmonica tube type, stamping type, and inflation type. Harmonica tube type cooling plates use twisted tubes to dissipate heat efficiently. Stamping type cooling plates are made by stamping metal sheets and provide a larger surface area for better cooling. Inflation type cooling plates have air inflated between metal sheets to enhance heat transfer. All these types aim to regulate battery temperatures effectively, ensuring optimal battery performance and longevity in vehicles.</p></p>
