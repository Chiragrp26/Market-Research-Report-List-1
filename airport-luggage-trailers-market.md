<p><p>The airport luggage trailers market consists of two main types: enclosed trailers and open trailers. Enclosed trailers are fully covered and provide maximum protection to the luggage from weather conditions and theft. They are commonly used for transporting high-value or fragile items. On the other hand, open trailers are not covered and offer easier access to the luggage. They are typically used for general luggage transportation, where weather conditions and security may not be major concerns. Both types cater to different needs of airlines and airports in efficiently managing the transportation of luggage.</p></p>
