<p><p>The suction-irrigation sets market offers three main types: disposable, semi-disposable, and reusable sets. Disposable sets are single-use and discarded after each procedure, ensuring sterile conditions but incurring higher costs. Semi-disposable sets can be used for multiple procedures before being discarded, striking a balance between cost and sterility. Reusable sets can be sterilized and used multiple times, reducing costs but requiring strict sterilization protocols. Each type caters to different needs and budgets, allowing healthcare facilities to choose the most suitable option based on their requirements.</p></p>
