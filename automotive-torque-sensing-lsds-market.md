<p><p>Automotive Torque Sensing Limited Slip Differentials (LSDs) are used to improve the traction and handling of vehicles. Cone-Type LSDs use a set of conical clutches to distribute torque between the wheels based on the difference in rotational speed. Plate-Type LSDs employ a stack of metal plates that restrict the rotation between the wheels, ensuring power distribution. Other types of LSDs include Torsen differentials, which use a gear train mechanism to transfer torque between wheels. All these LSDs help enhance vehicle performance and stability, especially in challenging road conditions or during high-speed maneuvers.</p></p>
