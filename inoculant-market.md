<p><p>The inoculant market comprises two main types: seed inoculants and soil inoculants. Seed inoculants are microbial formulations that coat the seeds before planting, helping to establish beneficial microorganisms on the seed surface and promote plant growth. Soil inoculants, on the other hand, are applied directly to the soil, containing microorganisms that enhance soil fertility and nutrient availability. Both types of inoculants play a crucial role in sustainable agriculture by improving plant health, yield, and overall crop productivity.</p></p>
