<p><strong>The PPS Resin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Linear Type</li><li>Cross-linked Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PPS resin is classified into two types: linear type and cross-linked type. The linear type refers to the PPS resin which has a linear molecular structure. This type offers excellent mechanical properties, high stiffness, and good dimensional stability, making it suitable for applications such as automotive parts and electrical components. On the other hand, cross-linked type PPS resin has a three-dimensional network structure, providing enhanced heat resistance, chemical resistance, and improved electrical insulation properties. This type is commonly used in fields like aerospace, electrical insulation materials, and industrial machinery due to its superior performance in extreme conditions.</p></p>
