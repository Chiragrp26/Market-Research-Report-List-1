<p><p>Nasal drug delivery systems encompass various market types, including nasal sprays, nasal powders, nasal drops, and nasal gels. Nasal sprays are liquid formulations administered through a pump or pressurized container. Nasal powders are finely ground particles delivered through insufflation devices. Nasal drops are liquid medications dispensed in drop form. Nasal gels are semisolid formulations applied to the nasal cavity. These different types offer diverse options for the delivery of drugs via the nasal route, providing targeted and efficient treatment for various ailments.</p></p>
