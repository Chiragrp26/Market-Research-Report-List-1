<p><p>Northern Bleached Softwood Kraft (NBSK) is a type of chemical pulp made from softwood fibers, primarily spruce, pine, and fir. NBSK pulp is generally produced in countries with abundant softwood resources, such as Canada, Sweden, Finland, and Russia.</p><p>The global NBSK market has been witnessing significant growth in recent years, driven by increasing demand for packaging and tissue papers. The packaging industry, in particular, has experienced a surge in demand for sustainable and eco-friendly packaging solutions, which has boosted the consumption of NBSK pulp. The growing e-commerce industry and the increasing preference for online shopping have also contributed to the market's growth, as the need for packaging materials and corrugated boards has increased.</p><p>Furthermore, the rising environmental concerns among consumers have led to a shift from plastics to paper packaging, further driving the demand for NBSK.
