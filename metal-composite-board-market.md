<p><p>Metal composite board, also known as metal composite panel, is a type of building material that consists of two metal sheets bonded to a thermoplastic core. The two metal sheets are usually made of aluminum, while the core can be made of materials such as polyethylene, mineral-filled thermoplastic, or fire-resistant materials. Metal composite boards are lightweight, durable, and have excellent thermal and acoustic insulation properties. They are widely used in the construction industry for applications such as external cladding, interior fit-outs, signage, and advertising.</p><p>The metal composite board market is witnessing significant growth due to the increasing demand for energy-efficient and aesthetically appealing building materials. The rising construction activities, particularly in emerging economies, are driving the demand for metal composite boards.
