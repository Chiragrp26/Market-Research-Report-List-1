<p><p>The Continuous Wave Laser Diode market can be divided into two main types: Type I and Type II. Type I refers to single wavelength laser diodes with a symmetric waveguide structure, while Type II includes laser diodes with a complex asymmetric waveguide structure emitting multiple wavelengths. These types differ in terms of their performance and applications. Type I laser diodes are commonly used in telecommunications, medical equipment, and industrial applications, while Type II laser diodes are preferred in sensing, spectroscopy, and other scientific research fields requiring varied wavelengths and high precision.</p></p>
