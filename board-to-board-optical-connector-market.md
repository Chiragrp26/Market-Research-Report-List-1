<p><p>The board to board optical connector market offers three main types of connectors: single row design, double row design, and triple row design. </p><p>In single row design, the connectors are arranged in a single row on both boards, providing a compact and space-saving solution. </p><p>Double row design features connectors arranged in two parallel rows on both boards, allowing more connections and higher data transfer rates. </p><p>Triple row design, with connectors arranged in three parallel rows on both boards, offers even more connections and enhanced data transmission capabilities. These variations cater to different application requirements in terms of space, connection density, and data transfer speed.</p></p>
