<p><p>The commercial amphibious vehicle market can be categorized into different types based on their propulsion systems. The screw propeller type is equipped with rotating blades that push water backward to generate forward motion. Water jet vehicles use a high-pressure jet of water to propel forward. Track-based amphibious vehicles use tracks similar to tanks to maneuver on land and water. The "others" category includes various propulsion methods like paddle wheels or air thrust. These different types cater to specific needs and preferences of consumers in the commercial amphibious vehicle market.</p></p>
