<p><p>Off-line LED drivers are power devices that regulate the electrical current flowing to LED lights, ensuring their proper operation. There are two main types of off-line LED drivers: DC power and AC power. DC power LED drivers accept a direct current input and convert it to the required voltage and current for the LED lights. AC power LED drivers, on the other hand, accept an alternating current input and convert it to the appropriate DC power needed for the LED lights. These drivers play a crucial role in enabling efficient and reliable lighting solutions.</p></p>
